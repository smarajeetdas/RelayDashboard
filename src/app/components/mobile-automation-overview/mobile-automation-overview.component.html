Overview

Mobile phones are gadgets characterized by applications that are unique in its quality requirements. While mobile applications were initially developed mostly in the entertainment sector, they are now touching more- critical domains intended to revolutionize the current payment system, to the m-government, and mobile-health initiative.

Relay Mobile Automation offers speed, coverage, and usability to Adobe Engg / Service teams. It will aid development team to de-risk pipelines and enable reusability of mobile devices across Adobe and deliver seamless experience. For more information please refer the Wiki.
